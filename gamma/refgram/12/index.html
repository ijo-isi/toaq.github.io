Toaq expresses aspect via aspectual verbs. They can be used as standalone verbs, but are usually found heading a verbal complex.

Aspectual
verb		 Meaning
chufaq “-ing”, “in the middle of” (PRG)
luı “has X-ed” (PRF)
za “about to”
hoaı “still”
haı “already”
he “in general” (gnomic)
